## Market Conditions

### Uptrend Definition
- Market consistently forming Higher Highs (HH) and Higher Lows (HL)
- Each swing high must be higher than previous swing high
- Each swing low must be higher than previous swing low

### Downtrend Definition  
- Market consistently forming Lower Lows (LL) and Lower Highs (LH)
- Each swing low must be lower than previous swing low
- Each swing high must be lower than previous swing high

### Consolidation Definition
- Market moving sideways without clear HH/HL or LL/LH pattern
- No significant net movement up or down over the analysis period

**NEW RULE**: Classify every candle as one of three types before trading:

**Strength Candles**: 
- Large candle body (>70% of total range)
- Small wicks (<20% of total range)
- Shows clear directional control by buyers or sellers
- **Action**: Trade in direction of strength candle

**Control-Shift Candles**:
- Large single wick (>60% of total range) either up or down
- Shows real-time takeover of control between buyers/sellers
- **Action**: First indication of potential reversal

**Indecision Candles**:
- Equal wicks on both sides
- Small body relative to wicks
- Shows no clear control by either buyers or sellers
- **Action**: Wait for breakout, don't trade directly

**Multi-Candle Momentum Analysis**:
- Compare speed of moves: Fast moves = stronger than slow moves
- Example: 1 hour up move vs 9 hours down = bullish strength dominance
